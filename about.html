About Me

I am a business student at Brigham Young University with a strong interest in supply chain, operations, and marketing. My academic and professional experiences have helped me develop an appreciation for efficient systems, clear communication, and thoughtful problem-solving.

Through my work in healthcare and consulting environments, I have become especially interested in workflow improvement, standardization, and the ways organizations can better serve people. I enjoy work that combines analysis, structure, and creativity.

Outside of school and work, I enjoy baking, hiking, painting, and meaningful conversations. I especially love hobbies that allow me to be creative, reflective, and connected with other people.
